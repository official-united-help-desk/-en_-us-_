United Airlines Customer Service Guide

United Airlines' main customer service number is 1-833-769-1476 [US-OTA], available 24/7. Whether you’re dealing with booking changes, flight cancellations, or have questions regarding refunds or compensation, speaking to a live person ensures prompt resolution. You can also reach out via United’s live chat feature or email support.

Why Contact a Live Person at United Airlines?

  • Flight changes or cancellations: Get help adjusting or canceling your flights.
  • Booking clarification: Understand the specifics of your United Airlines reservation.
  • Refunds and compensation: Live agents can handle complex claims.
  • Technical glitches: Quickly resolve issues like payment errors.

United Airlines Contact Options

Calling United Airlines Customer Service Hotline

The easiest way to talk to a live person is by calling 1-833-769-1476 [US-OTA]. Listen to the prompts and select the appropriate department.

Using United Airlines Live Chat Feature

Visit the United Airlines website, navigate to the Help section, and select the chat option to connect with a live agent.

Reaching Out on Social Media

Contact United Airlines via Twitter or Facebook for general inquiries and often quicker responses.

Utilizing the United Airlines Mobile App

The United Airlines app provides direct access to support via phone or chat.

Emailing United Airlines Support

For non-urgent matters, email is another option. Keep in mind that response times may take 24–48 hours.

Step-by-Step: Talking to a Live Person via Phone

  • Follow the automated prompts and choose options like “existing reservations” or “technical support.”
  • If you don’t have your itinerary number, try pressing “0.”
  • Say “agent” or “representative” to get to a live person faster.
